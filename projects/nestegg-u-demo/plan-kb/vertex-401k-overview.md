## What this plan is
The Vertex Manufacturing 401(k) is an employer-sponsored retirement savings plan. Eligible
employees contribute from their paycheck, Vertex adds a matching contribution, and the money is
invested and grows tax-advantaged until retirement. NestEgg is the recordkeeper — it holds the
accounts, runs the website and phone line, and provides education through NestEgg U.

## Who's eligible and how enrollment works
- **Eligibility:** employees age 21 or older can contribute **immediately** upon hire. The
  **employer match** begins after **6 months of service**.
- **Automatic enrollment:** new hires are enrolled at **4%** pre-tax unless they opt out or choose
  a different rate, with **automatic 1%/year increases up to 10%**.
- Change your rate, investments, or beneficiary anytime on the portal.

## Contributions
- **Pre-tax (traditional)** and **Roth (after-tax)** contributions are both allowed.
- **IRS annual limit:** $23,500 for 2025, plus a **$7,500 catch-up** if you're 50 or older
  (limits are set by the IRS and adjust each year).
- **Employer match:** **100% of the first 3%** you contribute, plus **50% of the next 2%** — so
  contributing **5%** earns the **full 4% match**.

## Vesting (what's yours to keep)
- **Your own contributions and their earnings are always 100% vested.**
- **Employer match** vests on a **3-year cliff**: 0% before 3 years of service, then **100% at 3
  years**. Leave before 3 years and you forfeit the unvested match.

## Investments
- The default investment (QDIA) is the **NestEgg Target Retirement** fund closest to your
  expected retirement year (e.g., NestEgg Target Retirement 2035).
- A **core menu** of index and actively managed funds is also available. You choose your mix on
  the portal.

## Fees
- **Recordkeeping/administrative fee:** about **$11 per quarter**, shown on your statement.
- Each fund also has its own **expense ratio** (published in the fund fact sheets on the portal).
