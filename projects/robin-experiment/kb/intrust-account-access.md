# INTRUST 401(k) Plan — Account Access & Login Help

*Source: 2025 INTRUST Enrollment Packet (Account Access sheet + How-To). Participant-facing summary
for Robin's Knowledge Base.*

> ⚠️ **Security rule for Robin (overrides this article):** the participant's website login uses
> their SSN, but **Robin verifies callers by Member ID + date of birth and must NEVER ask for,
> confirm, or repeat a Social Security Number, User ID, password, or one-time PIN aloud.** Describe
> *how* login works; never collect or read back a credential.

## Logging in online
- Access your account at **www.nesteggu.com/intrust** → select **Login** → choose **Participant**
  from the dropdown.
- **First-time login:** your default **User ID is your Social Security Number**, and your default
  **Password is the last 4 digits of your SSN**.
- Your **initial login credentials become invalid after 6 months** — if that happens, you must
  **contact NestEgg U**.
- On first login you will **change your User ID and Password**, verify/enter a mobile number and
  email, and **answer three security questions** (answers are case-sensitive).

## One-Time PIN (OTP)
- A **One-Time PIN is sent to your mobile phone or email** at login.
- Each OTP is **valid for 5 minutes**.
- **"Remember this device"** lets you skip the OTP for **90 days** on that same device.
- If **no delivery methods are listed**, or your preferred method is no longer valid, you must
  **contact NestEgg U** to receive a PIN.

## Mobile app
- Download the **NestEgg U App** and sign in with your current User ID and Password.
- **Fingerprint and Face ID** can be set up for secure, convenient access.
- The app lets you check balances, view investments, and change contributions.

## Getting help (live support)
- **Phone:** **866-412-9026** — live help **Monday–Friday, 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. Central**.
- Support can help with **resetting User IDs and Passwords** and **when you did not receive the PIN**,
  plus general account information and most transactions.
- **Email:** support@nesteggu.com
- **Note:** NestEgg U **cannot provide specific investment advice**.
