# INTRUST 401(k) Plan — Plan Overview

*Source: 2025 INTRUST Enrollment Packet (recordkept by NestEgg U). Participant-facing summary for
Robin's Knowledge Base. In any discrepancy, the Plan Document controls.*

## Eligibility & entry
- You are eligible to participate after **attaining age 18**.
- Your **Entry Date** is the **first day of the month** after you become eligible.

## Automatic enrollment
- Once eligible, if you do nothing you are **automatically enrolled deferring 6% of your pre-tax
  compensation** each pay period.
- **Automatic increase:** your deferral rises **1% each year on or about March 1**, until you reach
  **75%** of compensation, unless you elect otherwise.
- Auto-enrolled contributions are invested in the **BlackRock LifePath® target-date fund** closest
  to your Normal Retirement Age (the plan's default investment).
- You can **opt out** by completing the Custom Enrollment section of the **Election to Override
  Automatic Enrollment** form within **10 days** of your Entry Date. If you were auto-enrolled and
  want the money back, you have **90 days** from your first deferral to request a return.

## Your contributions
- You may contribute **1% to 75%** of eligible pay, in whole percentages.
- Choose **pre-tax deferrals**, **Roth 401(k) contributions**, or both. **Roth must be elected
  online** — it is not available by doing nothing.
- **"Bump It"** is the voluntary feature that increases your deferral **1% each year on ~March 1**.
- Annual IRS contribution and catch-up limits are in the **Saver section of NestEgg U**.
- **Change your contribution anytime:** online at www.nesteggu.com/intrust → My Account →
  Manage → Manage Account → **Change Contribution Rates**, or call **866-412-9026**.

## Employer match & other employer money
- **Safe harbor match:** the employer contributes **$1.00 per $1.00 you defer, up to 6%** of your
  compensation. This match is **100% vested immediately**.
- **3% nonelective profit-sharing contribution** is made to eligible employees who are **not active
  participants in the INTRUST Financial Corporation Employees' Retirement Plan**. This one is
  **subject to a vesting schedule**.

## Vesting
- You are **always 100% vested** in **your own salary deferrals, any rollover contributions, and all
  safe harbor matching contributions**.
- The **3% nonelective profit-sharing contribution** vests on a **3-year cliff**:
  **1 year = 0%, 2 years = 0%, 3 years = 100%**.
