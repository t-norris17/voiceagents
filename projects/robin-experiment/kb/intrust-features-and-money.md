# INTRUST 401(k) Plan — Loans, Withdrawals, Rollovers, Beneficiaries, Investments

*Source: 2025 INTRUST Enrollment Packet. Participant-facing summary for Robin's Knowledge Base. In
any discrepancy, the Plan Document / Summary Plan Description controls.*

## Loans
- The plan **allows participant loans**.
- There is a **$100 loan origination fee for each loan** you take from your account.
- ⚠️ **The enrollment packet does not state loan limits or repayment terms.** Robin should share
  what's known (loans are allowed; $100 fee) and **route specifics — how much, how long — to a
  specialist at 866-412-9026**, not guess.

## Withdrawals & distributions
- You generally **cannot withdraw deferrals or safe harbor contributions except on** severance from
  employment, **death**, or **disability**. See the Summary Plan Description for full rules.
- The plan also offers these flexibility features: **Hardship Withdrawals**, **In-Service
  Distributions**, and **In-Plan Roth Conversions**.
- **Military exception:** qualifying reservists/National Guard called to active duty may take a
  distribution of deferrals regardless of age, without the 10% early-distribution penalty.

## Rollovers (bringing money in)
- You **can roll your balance in from a former employer's plan**.
- Process: **review the Rollover Information packet**, **contact NestEgg U** (866-412-9026 /
  support@nesteggu.com) for help, **contact your previous plan's provider** to begin, and return the
  appropriate forms.

## Beneficiaries
- Set or change beneficiaries **online**: log in → **gear icon** (top-right) → **Beneficiaries**
  tab → view, add, or modify → **Save**. You can update anytime.
- **Primary** beneficiaries are first in line; **contingent** beneficiaries receive the balance only
  if all primaries predecease you.
- **Married participants:** if you are married and do **not** name your **spouse as 100% primary**,
  the designation is **void** — to name someone else, your **spouse must consent in writing and the
  signature must be notarized** (contact the Plan Administrator for the form).

## Investments
- **Default investment:** if you make no election, contributions go to the **age-appropriate
  BlackRock LifePath® target-date fund** for your Normal Retirement Age.
- **Change investments anytime:** log in → My Account → Manage → Manage Account → **Investment
  Election** tile, or call **866-412-9026**.
- **Self-Directed Brokerage (Charles Schwab PCRA):** you may open a self-directed account; the
  **initial transfer must be at least $1,000** and you may initially move **up to 90%** of your Plan
  balance.
- ⚠️ **Not advice:** NestEgg U **cannot provide specific investment advice** (which fund to pick).
  For guidance, INTRUST offers **Participant Investment Advice** (INTRUST is the Plan's Fiduciary
  Adviser) — contact **800-242-7111 ext. 1795** or **316-383-1795**.

## Fees (participant-relevant)
- **No asset-based administrative fee.**
- **Loan origination fee:** $100 per loan.
- **Participant Investment Advice fee** (only if you enroll in it): **4 basis points per month** of
  your account balance.

## Statements
- **Electronic statements** are available on the NestEgg U website for **18 months**.
- You may request a **free paper copy** by calling **866-412-9026**.
